## Key Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Founder burnout | High | AI drafts 70%, limit to 1-2h/day on X |
| Algorithm changes | Medium | Diversify: Telegram + Discord + email |
| Low testnet adoption | Medium | Incentivize with bounties + KOL amplification |
| Ambassador churn | Medium | Vest rewards, select quality over quantity |
