# Marketing Notes

## 📢 Positioning
ClawQuest is the "Tamagotchi for the AI Agency Era". It bridges the gap between static chatbots and autonomous agents that *do things* while you sleep.

## 🌟 Feature Bullets
- **Always On**: Your agent lives in the cloud, not on your phone.
- **Cross-Platform**: Check status on Web, interact via Telegram.
- **Real Progression**: Level up your agent to unlock harder quests and better loot.

## 🗣 Vocabulary
- **Don't say**: "Bot" (too generic). **Do say**: "Agent" or "Digital Companion".
- **Don't say**: "Script". **Do say**: "Quest Logic".

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Tech enthusiasts who want a low-maintenance "pet" project.
- Gamers interested in idle/incremental games.
- Crypto/Web3 natives (familiar with "ownership" of digital assets).
