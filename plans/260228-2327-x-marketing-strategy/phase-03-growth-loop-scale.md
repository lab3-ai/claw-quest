## Ambassador Program

### Structure

- **Size:** 5-10 active ambassadors (quality over quantity)
- **Compensation:** $200-400/month per ambassador + quest rewards
- **Tenure:** 3-month initial commitment, renewable

### Ambassador Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Monthly Pay | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Builder | 1k-10k | $200 | 4 posts/week, community replies |
| Influencer | 10k-50k | $300 | 3 posts/week, 1 thread/month |
| Alpha | 50k+ | $400 | 2 posts/week, 1 Spaces co-host/month |

### Recruitment Process

1. Identify candidates from testnet participants, engaged followers, AI/web3 community
2. DM with value prop: "Paid to talk about what you already care about"
3. Trial period: 2 weeks unpaid, demonstrate engagement quality
4. Formalize: sign simple agreement, set up payment (USDC or fiat)
5. Onboard: brand guidelines, content calendar access, private group chat

### Ambassador Deliverables

- **Minimum:** 2 posts/week mentioning ClawQuest (not forced — natural integration)
- **Monthly:** 1 thread or 1 Spaces co-host
- **Always:** Reply to ClawQuest posts, engage with community, provide feedback
- **Never:** Shill, make price predictions, attack competitors

## X Spaces Strategy

### Monthly Spaces Calendar

| Month | Topic | Format | Guests |
|-------|-------|--------|--------|
| Month 2, Week 1 | "State of AI Agents in 2026" | Panel (3 guests) | AI builders, researchers |
| Month 2, Week 3 | "Sponsor Roundtable" | AMA | 2-3 quest sponsors |
| Month 3, Week 1 | "Agent Competition Live" | Live demo | Top agents + owners |
| Month 3, Week 3 | "Building Onchain Trust" | Educational | Smart contract auditor + team |

### Spaces Playbook

1. Announce 48h before with teaser tweet
2. Reminder tweet 2h before
3. 2+ co-hosts always (team + guest)
4. Duration: 45-60 min
5. Record everything
6. Post-Spaces: clip 3-5 highlights → individual tweets next day
7. Summary thread: key takeaways (next morning)

## Paid Strategy (Ongoing)

### X Ads ($1,500/month)

| Allocation | Amount | Target |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Boost best-performing thread of the week | $600 | Engagement audiences |
| Promote weekly leaderboard | $400 | AI/web3 interests |
| Promote Spaces events | $300 | Follower lookalikes |
| Experimental / new format | $200 | Test new audiences |

**Rules:**
- Only boost posts that already have >500 organic impressions
- Kill underperforming ads within 24h
- A/B test 2 audiences per campaign
- Track: cost per follower, cost per testnet signup

### KOL Partnerships ($500/month)

- 1-2 selective partnerships per month
- Must be in AI/web3 niche (no generic crypto influencers)
- Must try product before posting
- Brief them with talking points, not scripts
- Track: impressions, engagement, attributed signups

## Monthly Review Process

### Week 1 of Each Month

1. Export analytics: Syntax + X native + UTM data
2. Review top 10 posts (what worked, why)
3. Review bottom 10 posts (what failed, why)
4. Analyze by content pillar: which pillar drives most engagement?
5. Check growth targets vs actuals
6. Survey: "How did you hear about ClawQuest?" (add to onboarding)
7. Ambassador performance review (posts, engagement, attitude)
8. Adjust content mix for next month
