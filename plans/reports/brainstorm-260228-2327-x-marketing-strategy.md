# Brainstorm: ClawQuest X Marketing Strategy

**Date:** 2026-02-28 | **Status:** Agreed

---

## Problem Statement

ClawQuest needs X (Twitter) marketing strategy to achieve: brand awareness, user acquisition, community building, investor/partner attention. Target audience: Web3/Crypto natives. Budget: >$5k/month. Founder-led with AI-assist content drafting. Brand account only (@ClawQuest).

## Constraints

- Product at v0.8.0 (MVP → Early Production)
- Testnet ready to deploy this week (Base Sepolia)
- No real users/case studies yet → build-in-public + thought leadership first
- Founder operates account (AI drafts 70%, founder edits 30%)
- Budget >$5k/month available

## Evaluated Approaches

### 1. Pure Build-in-Public
- **Pros:** Authentic, attracts builders/devs, low effort
- **Cons:** Slow growth, limited reach outside builder niche, doesn't attract sponsors
- **Verdict:** Good foundation but insufficient alone

### 2. Hype/KOL Campaign
- **Pros:** Fast follower growth, wide reach
- **Cons:** Expensive, low quality followers, not authentic, short-lived
- **Verdict:** Risky as primary strategy — crypto audience has KOL fatigue

### 3. Testnet Campaign Only
- **Pros:** Creates real engagement, product-as-proof
- **Cons:** One-shot, needs product ready, limited narrative
- **Verdict:** Great for Week 3-4 push but not sustainable alone

### 4. Mix Strategy (CHOSEN)
- Build-in-public + Thought leadership + Testnet campaign
- Phased rollout: foundation → launch → growth loop
- Meta-quest: use ClawQuest itself for marketing (product-market-fit proof)
- **Verdict:** Best balance of authenticity, reach, sustainability

## Final Recommended Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)
- 2-3 posts/day: threads, quote tweets, polls
- Build-in-public: code decisions, design choices, metrics, failures
- Thought leadership: AI agents + work, escrow trust, skill economy
- Strategic replies to 5-10 relevant posts daily
- NO links in main tweets (reach penalty) — links in replies only

### Phase 2: Testnet Launch (Week 3-4)
- Announcement thread + tutorial thread
- Meta-quest: "Promote ClawQuest on X → earn testnet USDC"
- Daily leaderboard updates (automated via API)
- X Spaces: live testnet walkthrough
- $1500 X Ads boost + $1000 micro-KOLs + $500 meme contest

### Phase 3: Growth Loop (Month 2-3)
- 3-5 posts/day with content pillars:
  - Build-in-public 25% | Thought leadership 20% | Product updates 20%
  - Memes 15% | Community spotlights 15% | Ambassador 5%
- Ambassador program: 5-10 micro-ambassadors ($200-400/person/month)
- Weekly metrics threads, monthly X Spaces

| Analytics (Syntax + Dash.so) | $250 | Performance tracking |

### Key Differentiators

1. **Meta-quest** — product markets itself (quest to promote ClawQuest = proof it works)
2. **Dual-audience narrative** — sponsors + AI agents = 2 marketing angles
3. **Onchain proof** — every payout has tx hash, transparent & verifiable
4. **Built-in content machine** — each quest completed = marketing asset

- Month 2: 7k followers, 4% engagement rate
- Month 3: 15k followers, 4-5% engagement rate

### X Algorithm Key Insights (2025-2026)

- Quote tweets > retweets (highest weight)
- Bookmarks > likes (stronger quality signal)
- Reply velocity first 60min = +40% distribution
- Links in tweets = -40% reach penalty
- Threads outperform single tweets 3-5x
- 3-5 posts/day optimal (>7 = spam flag)

### Mistakes to Avoid

- Constant shilling → shadowban
- Generic motivational content → spam
- Inconsistent posting → algorithm penalty
- Mega-influencer chasing → expensive, low ROI
- Overly salesy tone → crypto audience fatigue

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Founder burnout (3-5 posts/day) | AI draft 70%, founder edit 30%. Hire PT content person if needed |
| No real case studies yet | Build-in-public fills gap until testnet generates real data |
| KOL waste | Only selective micro-KOLs ($200-300), must actually try product |
| Algorithm changes | Diversify: also build Telegram + Discord + email list |
| Testnet bugs during campaign | QA thoroughly before launch campaign |
